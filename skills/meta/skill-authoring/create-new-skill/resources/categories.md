## Adding a new category

If nothing fits cleanly, don't shoehorn — open a `[category]` issue (see `.github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/category-proposal.yml`) or directly edit `categories.yml` in your PR. Categories are cheap to add; mis-categorised skills are expensive.
