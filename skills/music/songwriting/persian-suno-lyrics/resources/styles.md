# Per-style prosody contract

For each major Persian musical style, here is the contract a lyric must follow to sing well — not just read well.

For each: **register**, **line length**, **syllable density**, **melisma policy**, **consonant-cluster tolerance**, **chorus pattern**, **what to avoid**.

---

## پاپ (Pop)

- **Register**: colloquial.
- **Line length**: 6–10 syllables per line.
- **Syllable density**: medium. Verses can be conversational; chorus is sparser and more open.
- **Melisma**: minimal — at most one held vowel at the end of a chorus line.
- **Consonant clusters**: avoid clusters at line breaks; OK mid-line.
- **Chorus pattern**: 4 lines, identical syllable count, single hook word repeated, end-rhyme strict by ear.
- **Hook word candidates**: بیا، هنوز، تو، رو، دل، یه روز، چراغ.
- **Avoid**: register drift to formal mid-song, ezafe chains > 2, long abstract noun phrases.

---

## رپ / هیپ‌هاپ (Rap / Hip-Hop)

- **Register**: colloquial, sometimes street-flavoured (without slang AI can't pronounce).
- **Line length**: 12–16 syllables, often broken into 2–3 internal phrase units.
- **Syllable density**: high. Rhyme density also high — internal rhymes welcomed.
- **Melisma**: none.
- **Consonant clusters**: OK at line break (the rapper attacks them); avoid on the strong beat.
- **Chorus / hook**: 4 short lines, often 4–6 syllables each, with sustained-vowel hook.
- **Hook word candidates**: شب، دل، تنها، دور، باز، رو، یه روز.
- **Avoid**: melisma (kills rap), ezafe chains, formal verb forms (می‌رود → drop entirely, use می‌ره).
- **Rhyme rule**: every 2nd line at minimum; 4-line cluster rhymes preferred.

---

## راک (Rock)

- **Register**: semi-formal or colloquial — pick one and hold.
- **Line length**: 8–12 syllables, with a punchy attack syllable on beat 1.
- **Syllable density**: medium-high. Verses drive narrative.
- **Melisma**: rare; 1 chorus syllable held, never a sustained scream.
- **Consonant clusters**: encouraged — they give bite.
- **Chorus pattern**: 4 lines, declarative, hook word stays single-syllable for power.
- **Hook word candidates**: نه، رو، شب، خود، پاش، گم.
- **Avoid**: pop-style sweetness, soft endings, melisma over sustained chords.

---

## سنتی / آوازی (Traditional / Avazi)

- **Register**: formal.
- **Line length**: variable; often 7–11 syllables, sometimes long.
- **Syllable density**: low. Word weight high.
- **Melisma**: encouraged. Place on open vowels (â / i / u) at phrase ends, marked `~~` in lyrics.
- **Consonant clusters**: OK; AI handles them better at slow tempo.
- **Refrain pattern**: a single bayt or hemistich repeated, not a verse-chorus structure.
- **Hook concepts**: یار، دل، آرام، جان، یاد، بیا.
- **Avoid**: colloquial contractions («می‌رم» → wrong register), modern slang, urban-imagery words («خیابون», «پیام»).
- **Bonus**: pair with a known Persian classical poet's lexicon (Saadi or Hafez tier — see *Persian Poetry Composer* skill).

---

## تصنیف (Tasnif — composed traditional song)

- **Register**: formal or semi-formal.
- **Line length**: medium, with a clear bayt + refrain structure.
- **Syllable density**: medium.
- **Melisma**: yes, but disciplined — one or two per refrain.
- **Consonant clusters**: OK.
- **Form**: stanza + refrain (ترجیع), repeating refrain word verbatim.
- **Avoid**: free-verse looseness; tasnif demands form.

---

## بالاد سینمایی / تلفیقی (Cinematic ballad / Fusion)

- **Register**: semi-formal.
- **Line length**: 8–14 syllables.
- **Syllable density**: low to medium. Big breath room.
- **Melisma**: 1–2 places, chorus-end, on open vowels.
- **Consonant clusters**: OK if mid-line, never on a held note.
- **Chorus pattern**: 4–6 lines, slow-build, expansive vowel choices.
- **Hook word candidates**: بیا، هنوز، خونه، یار، دل، آرام.
- **Avoid**: rapid-fire syllables, slang, dense modern punctuation.

---

## آلترناتیو (Alternative)

- **Register**: colloquial or semi-formal — pick one.
- **Line length**: irregular but consistent within a section.
- **Syllable density**: medium, with deliberate gaps.
- **Melisma**: optional, sparse.
- **Consonant clusters**: OK; alternative tolerates rougher sounds.
- **Chorus pattern**: looser — can break the 4-line rule. Repetition by phrase rather than rhyme.
- **Hook**: often a phrase, not a single word ("هیچی نمونده", "همینه").
- **Avoid**: pop-formula chorus, cliché hooks.

---

## مینیمال شاعرانه (Minimal poetic)

- **Register**: semi-formal.
- **Line length**: short — 4–8 syllables.
- **Syllable density**: low. Lots of space.
- **Melisma**: optional, used as accent.
- **Consonant clusters**: avoid.
- **Form**: less verse-chorus structure; more stanza-and-pause.
- **Hook**: a recurring image or word, repeated 3+ times across the song.
- **Avoid**: filler words, redundant adjectives, conventional rhymes.

---

## الکترونیک (Electronic)

- **Register**: colloquial, often very sparse.
- **Line length**: 4–8 syllables.
- **Syllable density**: very low. Repeated short phrases work best.
- **Melisma**: none — let the production do the texture.
- **Consonant clusters**: avoid; the AI singer competes with synths.
- **Chorus pattern**: 1–2 lines repeated, often a single phrase.
- **Hook word candidates**: یه شب، تو، نگو، بمون، رو، حالا.
- **Avoid**: long narrative lines, complex syntax, formal register.

---

## نواحی / فولک (Regional / Folk)

- **Register**: depends on region — write معیار first, dialect-tag later.
- **Line length**: 6–10 syllables typically.
- **Syllable density**: medium.
- **Melisma**: yes, characteristic to many regional styles (Khorasani, Lori, Kurdish).
- **Consonant clusters**: tolerated.
- **Form**: bayt + refrain, often.
- **Avoid for AI**: writing directly in dialect orthography. Suno is unreliable on dialect spellings; use معیار, then dialect-tag in performance notes.

---

## نسل جدید (New generation — modern indie/pop)

- **Register**: colloquial.
- **Line length**: short, conversational — 5–9 syllables.
- **Syllable density**: low. Lots of breath.
- **Melisma**: rare, accent only.
- **Consonant clusters**: avoid in chorus; OK mid-verse.
- **Chorus pattern**: short, punchy hook (often 2 lines repeated).
- **Hook word candidates**: تو، رو، حالا، هنوز، دیگه.
- **Avoid**: faux-classical posturing, archaic vocabulary, long narrative buildups.

## Quick decision matrix

| If user says…                                        | Default style                  | Default register |
|------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------|------------------|
| "ballad", "emotional", "love song"                   | Cinematic ballad               | semi-formal      |
| "pop", "catchy", "earworm"                           | Pop                            | colloquial       |
| "rap", "hip-hop", "verse with bars"                  | Rap                            | colloquial       |
| "rock", "with guitar", "energetic"                   | Rock                           | colloquial or semi-formal |
| "traditional", "classical", "آواز"                   | Traditional / Avazi            | formal           |
| "tasnif", "with refrain"                             | Tasnif                         | formal           |
| "fusion", "Iranian + modern"                         | Fusion                         | semi-formal      |
| "alternative", "indie", "weird"                      | Alternative                    | colloquial       |
| "minimal", "poetic", "sparse"                        | Minimal poetic                 | semi-formal      |
| "electronic", "ambient", "trip-hop"                  | Electronic                     | colloquial       |
| "modern", "indie pop", "نسل جدید"                    | New generation                 | colloquial       |
| "Khorasani / Lori / Kurdish folk"                    | Regional folk (معیار + tag)    | formal or semi   |

If the user names two styles ("ballad with rap verse"), pick the one carrying the chorus and let the other inflect a single section.
