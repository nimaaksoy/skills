# Persian song clichés — the noise floor

Every word and image below has appeared in so many Persian songs that, by itself, it now reads as filler. The listener hears the phrase as wallpaper — present, not registered.

**Max one cliché phrase per song.** If you use one, anchor it to a specific image the listener hasn't met before. *"چراغ خونه روشنه"* on its own is filler. *"چراغ خونه روشنه چون هنوز برق رو به نام تو نوشتم"* earns it.

---

## Chorus-line clichés (avoid)

These are entire lines that could open or close half the love songs ever written in Persian:

- چراغ خونه روشنه
- صدای تو هنوز اینجاست
- ردِّ پای تو
- این کوچه‌های خیس
- شبِ تاریک و تنها
- قلبِ شکسته‌ی من
- بدون تو زندگی بی‌معنیه
- چقدر دلم برات تنگ شده
- اشک‌های من بی‌رحمن
- ستاره‌ی بی‌سو
- پنجره‌ی بسته
- ساعتِ بی‌رحم
- جاده‌ی بی‌انتها
- بارانِ بی‌امان
- آسمونِ گریون
- خاطره‌های دور
- دستای خالی من
- این شب به آخر نمی‌رسه
- روزای رفته
- اون لحظه‌ای که رفتی

If your draft includes more than one of these, the song is generic by construction.

---

## Cliché image pairs (avoid as combinations)

Each word is fine on its own. Combined, they make wallpaper:

- شب + باران
- دل + شکسته
- چشم + اشک
- آسمون + ستاره
- جاده + تنها
- پنجره + سکوت
- شمع + خاموش
- صدا + خاطره
- چراغ + خاموش
- کوچه + قدیمی

If two of these pairs appear in the same verse, rewrite.

---

## Words you can almost always cut

These rarely earn their place. They're filler that the model adds to hit syllable count:

- یه جوری
- یه چیزی
- همه چی
- هیچی
- یه روزی (without a specific follow-up)
- چقدر (when used as emotional intensifier)
- خیلی (when used as filler)
- آخه
- خب
- دیگه (when not contrastive)

---

## Pronoun anaphora warning

Watch for «اون» (he/she/it/that) used as a placeholder when the antecedent is vague. *"اون لحظه‌ای که..."* is fine if the moment is then named. *"اون چیزی که گفتی..."* without a specific reference is filler.

---

## Verb clichés

Verb pairings that have stopped carrying weight:

- رفتی و نموندی
- شکستی منو
- تنهام گذاشتی
- یادم نمیره (without context)
- دلتنگتم
- بی‌قرارم
- می‌سوزم

Replace with a specific action: *"کلید رو روی میز گذاشتم نرفتم سراغش"* beats *"رفتی و نموندی"*.

---

## How to test for cliché

Before delivering, take any line and Google it (or imagine if it would return many results). If the line is on a thousand Spotify previews, the listener has nothing new to hear. Rewrite with a specific image rooted in your Step 1 persona.

---

## When a cliché is OK

A cliché works when:

1. It's the *one* allowed in the song (budget: 0–1).
2. It's anchored to a specific, fresh image in the same line or the next.
3. It's said in a voice that owns it (a specific persona reframing it).

Bad: *"چراغ خونه روشنه / ولی صدا فقط منم"* — two clichés in two lines, no anchor.

OK: *"چراغ خونه روشنه چون هنوز برق رو به اسمِ تو نوشتم"* — one cliché ("چراغ خونه روشنه"), then a concrete specific anchor (the utility bill in your name). The cliché earns its place because the second clause makes it specific.
