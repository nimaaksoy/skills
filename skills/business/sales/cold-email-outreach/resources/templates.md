# Cold email openers

Three reusable openers. Replace the bracketed parts with one specific, true fact about the recipient.

## 1. Recent news opener

> Saw [the specific event] last [week / month]. [One sentence about what that usually means operationally for someone in their role].

Example fill-in:

> Saw the Series B announcement last week. The first ninety days after a raise are usually when revops realises the old reporting stack does not survive the new headcount.

## 2. Product change opener

> Noticed [the specific product change] shipped recently. [One sentence about the second-order problem it tends to create].

Example fill-in:

> Noticed the new self-serve plan shipped last month. Self-serve usually triples support volume before the help docs catch up.

## 3. Shared context opener

> [Mutual person] mentioned you were thinking about [specific topic]. [One sentence connecting that topic to a problem you understand well].

Example fill-in:

> Sara mentioned you were thinking about moving billing in-house. The migration itself is straightforward; the part teams underestimate is the tax mapping for the first international customers.
